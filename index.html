Controls

Use arrow keys to move.

Power Ups

Keep the tree alive to earn rewards of apples to spend on power ups.
  • Press 1: Speed power up (30 apples)
  • Press 2: Jump power up (10 apples)
  • Press 3: More Rain power up (50 apples)
